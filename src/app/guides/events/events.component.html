Giftmas (December/January)

Ghosts (1 in 48 from any monster, none offline) - presents from Giftmas Blobulyte (15 per quest), farm monsters for 40 per day, or infinite from ghosts (can come from AFK gains both skilling and fighting). Santa Hat from 1 in 50 that increases to 1 in 300 after the first from presents. Cards can come from presents except ghost card which comes from ghosts. Giftmas foods

Valentines (February/March)

All monsters drop Lovey Dovey Letters, which are both needed for the Loveulyte quest line, and can also be used to summon Valentslimes.

Additionally, all monsters drop Choco Boxes, which can be opened for a chance at the Giant Bow Hat, as well as the holiday food (Cherry Choco, Minto Choco, Milk Choco, Lemony Choco).
