Valentines (February/March)

For the duration of the event, enemies have a chance to drop Lovey Dovey Letters. 25 are needed each day for the Loveulyte daily quest line.

The Loveulyte is located in the Blunderhills town, and has a two part daily quest line that can be completed once per account, and resets when the shops reset. 75 Lovey Dovey Letters are needed to complete the entire quest line. Completing both parts gives 30 Choco Boxes, 20 Gems, and 1x 1 Hr Time Candy each day.

Additionally, Lovey Dovey Letter can be used to spawn Valentslimes, of which 20 need to be defeated each day for the 2nd part of the Loveulyte daily quest line. It is recommended to farm for as many letters, and to spawn as many Valentslimes as possible so you can have the best chance at completing all of the cards and collecting a large amount of event food.
